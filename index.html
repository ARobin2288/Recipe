Puff Pastry Pulled Pork

This quick and easy recipe is sure to impress all your party guests!

Ingredients:

  • 1 defrosted package puff pastry
  • ¾ pound pulled pork
  • ¾ cup BBQ sauce
  • 1¼ cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 egg beaten
  • 1 Tbsp water

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400ºF
  2. Line baking sheets with parchment paper. Set aside
  3. Unfold pastry sheets. Cut each sheet into nine squares
  4. Toss pulled pork with BBQ sauce
  5. Arrange pulled pork and cheese down center of the pastry squares
  6. Fold two opposite corners of each square over the filling and press edges to seal
  7. Place on prepared baking sheet
  8. Combine egg and water
  9. Brush each pastry with egg wash
  10. Bake 15-18 minutes, until golden brown
  11. Serve warm or at room temperature
